## ⏱️ **[0:50 – 1:20] – Registration Flow**

**Narrator (show login screen in video):**
"Let's start with registration.

The rider logs in using their phone number and OTP.

After verification, the rider completes a **5-step onboarding**:

* Basic info like age and income
* Location and working zone
* Vehicle type
* Risk-related questions

Based on this data, the system instantly creates a personalized insurance policy."

What makes this powerful is our **AI-based premium calculation**:

* It considers income level
* Zone risk
* Seasonal factors
* Rider behavior

This ensures **fair and personalized pricing** instead of fixed premiums."

## ⏱️ **[1:50 – 2:40] – Claims & Automation (Core Feature)**

**Narrator (show claims flow / trigger in video):**
"Now the most important part — **automated claims**.

When a disruption happens, like heavy rain:

* The system automatically detects it
* Creates a claim instantly
* Runs a **4-layer fraud check**:

  * GPS verification
  * Weather validation
  * Earnings analysis
  * Pattern detection

If verified, the payout is calculated using our formula and sent instantly.

This entire process takes **less than a minute**, unlike traditional insurance."

## ✅ **Tips for Video Recording**

### Visual Cues
- **[0:00-0:20]** - Show title slide + problem statement graphics
- **[0:20-0:50]** - Display system architecture diagram
- **[0:50-1:20]** - Screen recording of login → OTP in terminal → onboarding flow
- **[1:20-1:50]** - Show policy dashboard with different tiers, premium calculation demo
- **[1:50-2:40]** - Screen recording of claim trigger, fraud detection running, payout confirmation
- **[2:40-3:00]** - Closing slide with key metrics and thank you

### Recording Tips
- Speak at 130–150 words/min (perfect for 3-minute duration)
- Use clear, concise language
- Sync narration with video playback
- Add captions for key terms (OTP, AI, fraud detection, etc.)
- Include B-roll: app screenshots, charts, terminal output

### Audio Elements
- Use background music (soft, non-intrusive)
- Add sound effects for transitions (optional)
- Ensure microphone quality is high
- Use professional narration (consider voice actors for polish)

### Video Editing
1. **Timestamp accuracy** - Match narration with video scenes
2. **Transitions** - Keep smooth between sections
3. **Graphics** - Add callouts for technical terms
4. **Text overlays** - Show key numbers (e.g., "<60 seconds to payout")
5. **Color grading** - Keep consistent with GigShield brand
